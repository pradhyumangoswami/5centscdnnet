Speed

With Delivery Accelerate, you can expect lightning-fast speeds for your static and dynamic assets such as style sheets, JavaScript files, and images.

Minimization: Delivery Accelerator minimizes and optimizes your website’s digital asset such as style sheets, JavaScript files, and images.

Available all-over-the globe: Using CDN, delivery accelerator stores your website in all of its available POPs (point of presence servers).

When a user requests to access your website, the CDN's nearest server to that user will deliver the website's content. This means that your website's data doesn't have to travel all the way from your server to the user's computer, reducing the time it takes to load your pages.
